My Artistic Background

Art Mediums

These are Mediums that I work with when creating my artwork.

  • Paper and ink
  • Colored Pencils and Marker
  • Digital Art

Working with pen and ink has always been a special passion of mine. There’s something so satisfying about creating different brushstrokes, each one carrying its own energy and character. The feeling of buying a fresh sketchbook fills me with excitement, a blank canvas ready for new ideas and experiments. I love the permanence of the medium—the fact that mistakes can't be erased makes every mark intentional, even when things don't go as planned. It’s a freeing way to create, where every imperfection becomes part of the artwork's story.

I love combining colored pencils and markers in my creative process. I start with markers to lay down a bold, smooth base, and then layer colored pencils over them to add texture and intricate details. Vibrant colors are my go-to, as they bring so much energy and personality to my work. This technique allows me to blend the boldness of markers with the subtlety of pencil strokes, transforming the ideas in my imagination into vivid, tangible pieces of art. It’s a process that feels dynamic and deeply satisfying every time.

Creating digital art on Procreate and Adobe has become one of my favorite creative outlets. The possibilities feel truly endless in this medium, with countless tools and effects at my fingertips to bring my ideas to life. I also love how accessible it is—I can take my tablet anywhere, making it easy to create while traveling or on the go. Whether experimenting with new techniques or perfecting a piece, digital art offers a freedom and versatility that keeps me inspired. The source of supplies at Micheals.

My inspirations

Ian Worthington
Ian Worthington, better known as Worthikids on Instagram and YouTube, is an artist I’ve admired since I discovered his work back in 2018. At the time, he was sharing short animations, and I was instantly captivated by his unique style. His characters are full of life, with expressions that feel so genuine and relatable. Beyond that, he’s a master of many talents, from animation to music, showcasing an impressive versatility. What truly sets him apart is his sense of humor, which shines brilliantly through his creations, making them not only entertaining but also deeply memorable.
Criag McCracken
Craig McCracken is one of my all-time favorite artists. I grew up watching his incredible cartoons, with The Powerpuff Girls, Foster’s Home for Imaginary Friends, and Kid Cosmic holding a special place in my heart. Not only do these shows evoke a strong sense of nostalgia, but they’ve also stood the test of time, as I still enjoy them as an adult. His work is a perfect mix of sweetness and depth, with unforgettable characters and stories. I deeply respect him for his talent and creativity as a cartoon maker—his impact on animation is undeniable.
bluffycr3am
Bluffycr3am’s digital artwork never fails to leave me in awe. Her talent and creativity are at a level I aspire to reach someday. Beyond being an inspiration, I see her as a role model, especially because she’s from Mexico, which gives me an extra sense of connection to her journey. What truly amazes me is her ability to consistently produce such high-quality work at an incredible pace. Her dedication and passion shine through everything she creates, and it’s one of the many reasons I think she’s an amazing person.
